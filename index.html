Mantis Predict is a data-driven marketing and customer analytics consultancy founded by Aman Undirwade, focused on helping businesses make clear, confident decisions using real data.

The firm was created to solve a common problem seen across startups and growing companies: data exists, dashboards exist, reports exist — but clarity is missing. Decisions are often made based on assumptions, surface-level metrics, or overwhelming dashboards that look impressive but don't guide action.

Mantis Predict takes a decision-first approach. Instead of building dashboards for show, the focus is on understanding customer behavior, campaign performance, and business outcomes — and translating that data into insights leaders can actually use.

The consultancy works at the intersection of marketing, analytics, and decision-making, helping teams understand what is happening, why it is happening, and what to do next.

Mantis Predict operates as a registered business in India and follows transparent, compliant practices.
